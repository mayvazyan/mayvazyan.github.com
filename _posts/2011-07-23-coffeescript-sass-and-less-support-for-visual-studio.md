It's a wonderfully clean integration.</p>  <p><img src="http://www.hanselman.com/blog/content/binary/Windows-Live-Writer/cde67c1bbe8b_C03B/MvcApplication47%20-%20Microsoft%20Visual%20Studio%20(110)_thumb.png" /></p>
